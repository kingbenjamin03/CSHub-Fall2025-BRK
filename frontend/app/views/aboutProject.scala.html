About SMU-Lyle-Sci-Hub Portal

@*
Wish Overview
*@

SMU Scientific Hub (SMU-Lyle-Sci-Hub) platform aims to support scientific/business collaboration, knowledge sharing and research between SMU and local industry in Dallas-Fort Worth Metropolitan Area. Sci-Hub is powered by the most recent computer science techniques including machine learning/deep learning, artificial intelligence, text mining/information retrieval, knowledge graph, semantic web, natural language processing, cloud computing, and services computing techniques. In its first phase, we will focus on collaborations on computer science and related fields.

SMU-Lyle Scientific Hub is featured with the following core capabilities:


1. Find SMU researchers for your business - SMU World of Talent at Your Fingertips

Let us know your challenges/issues/wishes, SMU-Lyle-Sci-Hub will utilize AI/Machine Learning techniques to help you find SMU researchers (maybe in a team) with appropriate expertise to provide research solution services.

SMU-Lyle-Sci-Hub aims to help local industry find SMU researchers with appropriate expertise to help solve their challenges/issues and to provide corresponding research services.


2. Find challenges/issues needed in industry

SMU-Lyle-Sci-Hub aims to help SMU faculty/students find and understand the needs from the local industry.


3. Provide an open collaboration platform

SMU-Lyle-Sci-Hub aims to provide an open platform supporting and facilitating the collaboration among SMU researchers with the local industry.


4. Window to SMU Technology

SMU-Lyle-Sci-Hub will help industry understand the latest exciting technology invented by SMU faculty and students.


5. A Community-Driven Hub

With a full understanding of the needs of university/industry collaboration, SMU-Lyle-Sci-Hub makes it easy for community users to suggest functions and wishes to help build the hub together.

@*

2. Scientist Social Network

The platform will serve as a "free will host a virtual scientist social network, where scientists form various types of relationships based on their co-authorship.


3. Wish

SMU-Hub allows community users to register and request research services or help. Wishes signify community users' interests and help shape community needs for research.


4. Virtual Assistant

SMU-Hub provides AI-powered virtual assistant that allows you to talk about your needs and provides you support in voice.


5. Intelligent Recommendations

Papers can be easily uploaded, shared, organized, searched, and recommended like photos and videos on the YouTube.
